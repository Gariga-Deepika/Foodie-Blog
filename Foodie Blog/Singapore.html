4. Chili Crab - Singapore

CHILLI CRAB - THE SINGAPORE "MUST - TRY" SPECIALITY Renowned for its authentic and delicate flavors, delight your taste buds with delectable Singapore Style Chili Crab. Experience a perfect blend of succulent crab meat with sweet and savory red-tangy sauce.

This spicy chili crab, drenched in a spicy gravy of tomato and chili. This dish is a local bestseller, and people like it a lot. The spicy gravy of the dish is best for spicy lovers, and it is served with buns.
