12. Croissant, France






In France, croissants are generally sold without filling and eaten without added butter, but sometimes with almond filling. In the United States, sweet fillings or toppings are sometimes used, and warm croissants may be filled with ham and cheese, or feta cheese and spinach.

It is a famous flaky pastry, prepared with butter and dough and topped with raspberry jam. The pastry is soft and amazing breakfast food. Coffee and croissant together make a great couple. The recipe is quite easy, and you should give it a try. These pastries are love.croissant
