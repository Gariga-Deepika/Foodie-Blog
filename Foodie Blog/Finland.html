2. Squeaky Cheese - Finland





Squeaky cheese is a must-try when you visit Finland. It is a specialty of Finland and is usually pan-fried and often served with cloudberry jam or coffee. The texture of the dish is soft and cheesy, and the presentation is enough to satisfy your eyes, and the taste is heavenly. The cloudberry jam enhances its flavor.

The Complete Idiot's Guide to Cheeses of the World calls juustoleipa “one of the most unusual cheeses on the planet.” And if there is anything I love, it is an unusual cheese. Juustoleipä is a Finnish word that translates to “cheese bread,” which further increases its appeal as the holy union of two of my favorite food groups.
