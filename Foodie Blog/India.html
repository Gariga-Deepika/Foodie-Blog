14. Masala Dosa - India







Masala Dosa, also called Masale dosa, is a South Indian dish. It is a type of dosa and has its origin in the town of Udupi in Karnataka. It is made from rice, lentils, Urad dal, Chana dal, fenugreek, puffed rice, Toor dal, dry red chilli and served with potato curry, chutneys, and sambar.

Masala dosa is a famous dish in India. It is prepared by using rice batter and filled with mashed potatoes. It is served with coconut chutney, sambar (a type of curry), and some sauces. The dish is amazing for breakfast, and the crispiness of the dosa is amazing.
